About the Joint Venture

Pluribus Polaris JV is a strategic joint venture formed to serve federal civilian and defense agencies under the GSA Polaris Government-Wide Acquisition Contract (GWAC). By combining the specialized expertise of our partner firms, we deliver end-to-end IT services that help agencies achieve their modernization goals.

Our joint venture brings together proven past performance, deep domain knowledge, and a shared commitment to mission success. We are positioned to support task orders across all Polaris functional areas, offering agencies a trusted, high-capability partner from day one.
